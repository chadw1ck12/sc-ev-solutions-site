Common questions

The quick answers homeowners usually want before booking a Level 2 charger install.

Do I need a specific charger brand?

No. Upstate EV Charger Solutions can install many common residential Level 2 chargers, including Tesla wall connectors and universal chargers for other EVs.

Can every home support a Level 2 charger?

Most homes can, but the panel capacity and available breaker space need to be reviewed first. If upgrades are needed, you’ll know before moving forward.

Is hardwiring better than using an outlet?

Often yes for long-term reliability and higher amperage setups, but the right choice depends on your charger, electrical setup, and goals.

How long does installation take?

Many straightforward residential installs can be completed in a single visit, while more complex jobs may take longer depending on scope.
